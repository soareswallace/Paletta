Privacy Policy · Last updated April 2026

Paletta does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything happens on your device.

Camera

Paletta uses your camera to extract color palettes in real time. Camera frames are processed entirely on-device and are never saved, transmitted, or shared.

Data Collection

We collect nothing. Paletta has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no third-party SDKs. There is no data to delete because no data is ever stored.

Third Parties

Paletta contains no third-party code, advertising networks, or analytics libraries. No data is sent to any external server.

Children

Paletta does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
